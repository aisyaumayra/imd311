FATHER

Allow me to introduce my father, Muizzudin bin Mohd Haris, a dynamic individual who, at the age of 39, continues to make significant contributions as a dedicated government servant at KPDNKK. Beyond his professional pursuits, his life is interwoven with a profound passion for Futsal. In his younger days, he not only showcased exceptional skill as the ex-captain of Futsal Malaysia but has gracefully transitioned into the role of a coach as the years have unfolded. Witnessing his journey from a spirited player to a mentor reflects not only his enduring love for the sport but also the wisdom he imparts to the next generation of athletes. Muizzudin's commitment to Futsal serves as an inspiring testament to his resilience and unwavering dedication to the game that has become an integral part of his identity.
